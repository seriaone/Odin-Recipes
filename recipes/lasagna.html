Cheesy Lasagna

Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 (26 ounces) can spaghetti sauce
  • 1 pound cottage cheese
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. In a blender or with an electric mixer, blend tomato sauce and cottage cheese together until smooth.
  4. Spoon a little of the sauce mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish.
  5. Place a layer of cooked noodles over the sauce, and sprinkle a portion of the mozzarella, the cheddar and the parmesan over the noodles.
  6. Repeat layering of sauce, noodles and cheese; finish with a cheese layer.
  7. Bake in preheated oven 30 to 45 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.
